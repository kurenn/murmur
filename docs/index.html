Privacy by architecture

Your voice never leaves your laptop.

Most dictation tools stream your microphone to someone else's servers. Murmur doesn't. Transcription and the AI cleanup pass both run locally — so the most personal data you produce all day stays on the one machine you control.

Whisper runs on your CPU or GPU. Audio is transcribed in memory and discarded — never written to disk, never sent anywhere.

No account, no cloud

No sign-up, no sync, no telemetry, no background phone-home. Download it, grant mic access, and start talking.

Open source, forever
